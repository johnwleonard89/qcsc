Project Overview

This luxury automotive dealership needed a showroom floor that would match the premium quality of their vehicles. The existing concrete floor was detracting from the customer experience and failing to properly showcase their high-end inventory.

Our ultra high-gloss epoxy system created a mirror-like finish that transforms the space into a luxury showcase environment. The reflective surface enhances lighting, creates depth, and provides the perfect backdrop for vehicle displays while maintaining easy maintenance and exceptional durability.

Installation Timeline

  • Friday Evening - Pre-Work

    Vehicle Removal & Protection

    Coordinated vehicle relocation and comprehensive masking of surrounding surfaces, fixtures, and equipment.

  • Saturday - Day 1

    Surface Preparation

    Diamond grinding to achieve perfect smoothness, crack repair, and thorough degreasing. Applied moisture-blocking primer system.

  • Saturday Night - Day 2

    Base Coat Application

    Applied high-build epoxy base coat with precision flow and leveling for ultra-smooth foundation layer.

  • Sunday - Day 3

    High-Gloss Finish

    Applied ultra high-gloss topcoat system and performed final quality inspection. Vehicles repositioned Sunday evening.
