Project Overview

This busy downtown restaurant required a complete kitchen flooring overhaul to meet updated health department standards and handle the demands of 200+ daily covers. The existing concrete floor had developed cracks and stains that posed serious hygiene risks and made cleaning extremely difficult.

Our NSF-certified epoxy system provided the perfect solution - creating a seamless, non-porous surface that meets all food safety requirements while withstanding the harsh conditions of commercial kitchen operations including high temperatures, chemical exposure, and constant foot traffic.

Challenges & Solutions

🚫 Hygiene Compliance Issues

Cracked concrete harbored bacteria and made thorough sanitization impossible, risking health department violations.

✅ NSF-Certified Surface

Installed seamless, non-porous epoxy system certified for direct food contact, eliminating bacterial harboring points.

🚫 Chemical Damage

Daily exposure to harsh cleaning chemicals and degreasers was deteriorating the concrete surface.

✅ Chemical Resistance

Applied specialized chemical-resistant topcoat that withstands industrial cleaners, degreasers, and sanitizers.

🚫 Operational Downtime

Restaurant couldn't afford extended closure during their peak season operations.

✅ Off-Hours Installation

Completed entire installation during off-hours over 3 nights, allowing normal business operations to continue.
