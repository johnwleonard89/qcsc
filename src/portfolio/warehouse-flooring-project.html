Project Overview

This 45,000 square foot distribution center required a complete flooring overhaul to handle increased operational demands. The facility processes over 1,000 pallets daily with constant forklift traffic, heavy machinery, and aggressive cleaning protocols.

Our industrial-grade epoxy system was specifically engineered to withstand point loads up to 50,000 lbs, resist chemical spills, and provide a seamless surface that enhances safety and operational efficiency while reducing maintenance costs by 75%.

Installation Timeline

  • Days 1-3

    Surface Preparation Phase

    Complete shot blasting to SSPC-SP6 commercial blast standard. Removal of all contamination, oil stains, and weak concrete. Crack injection and structural repairs.

  • Days 4-5

    Primer & Base System

    Applied penetrating epoxy primer followed by steel fiber-reinforced base coat. Moisture vapor barrier installation in high-traffic areas.

  • Days 6-8

    Build Coat Application

    Multiple high-build epoxy layers with integrated slip-resistant aggregates. Each coat applied with 16-hour minimum recoat window.

  • Days 9-10

    Topcoat & Quality Control

    Final polyurethane topcoat application with line striping. Comprehensive adhesion testing and final quality inspection before handover.
